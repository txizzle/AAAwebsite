Survive the Night

Cal's largest street race

Hey Everyone!!! We know you've been waiting, so we are delivering. UC-Berkeley's largest, most epic and thrilling, hybrid game of Capture the Flag and Tag is back! So get your team ready (6 people max) or sign up to be a chaser (capped) >:)

LANB4TIME

Cal's largest LAN party

Welcome to AAA's Fall 2014 LANB4Time! We are back to bring you Berkeley's biggest and best video game party. This year, we are proud to be working together with Smash At Berkeley and Berkeley Legends to be bringing you tournaments in League of Legends, Project M, and Melee. We also will be hosting a Hearthstone event. Rooms will also be available for anyone to come and just play any game casually or just hangout. Hope to see you all there!

Family Night

Come meet our lovely parents

Everyone is welcome to join a family. The parents of the families will be hosting events such as family dinners, ice skating, study party, karaoke and much more! There is no commitment, so you can join anytime and go to the events you want. Come out for an unforgettable night of LAUGHTER, EXCITEMENT, FREE FOOD, and NEW FRIENDSHIP!!!
